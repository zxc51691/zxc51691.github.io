STEP 2

Stance and Posture

With your feet set, you’ll want to lower your center of gravity by bending your knees. Crouching down a little prepares you for the jump and gives you more power when you spring off the ground.

Imagine you’re about to leap into the air, you’ll want your knees to be bent enough to explode upward when you pop the tail. This part is crucial because it sets you up to jump in sync with the board. Don’t rush the crouch; take your time to balance yourself and get ready to pop the board properly.
