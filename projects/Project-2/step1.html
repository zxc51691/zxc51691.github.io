STEP 1

FOOT POSITIONING

Before you even think about popping the board, make sure your feet are in the right place. Start by placing your back foot squarely on the tail of the skateboard. Your toes should rest comfortably across the width of the board, giving you enough leverage for a strong pop.

GRAPHIC FPO

Your front foot should be placed slightly behind the front bolts, angled just a bit, and positioned so you can easily slide it up the board. This setup gives you balance and control over the board. Spend time adjusting your feet until it feels right, as solid foot placement is key to a successful ollie.
